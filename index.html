Corn flour production from farm to shop

1
Harvest on farm

Vocabulary

harvest, crop, yield, collect, load

Model sentence

Initially, mature corn is harvested on farms before being transported to a processing facility.

2
Washing

Vocabulary

rinse, cleanse, debris, impurities, husk removal

Model sentence

Upon arrival at the factory, the corn is washed to remove husk and other impurities.

3
Grinding

Vocabulary

crush, grind, break down, fragments, particles

Model sentence

The cleaned corn is then ground into smaller fragments for further processing.

4
Milling

Vocabulary

refine, mill, pulverise, fine powder, consistent texture

Model sentence

Following this, the material is milled to produce a fine and consistent powder.

5
Sifting

Vocabulary

sift, screen, filter, coarse particles, residue

Model sentence

The flour is subsequently sifted to eliminate larger particles and ensure smoothness.

6
Packaging

Vocabulary

package, seal, label, container, batch

Model sentence

Once refined, the flour is packaged into sealed containers ready for distribution.

7
Distribution

Vocabulary

distribute, deliver, retail outlets, supply chain, logistics

Model sentence

Finally, the finished product is distributed to shops and restaurants for consumer use.
